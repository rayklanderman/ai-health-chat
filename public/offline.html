üí° Basic First Aid Tips

  • Choking: Stand behind the person and perform the Heimlich maneuver by giving abdominal thrusts.
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure to the wound with a clean cloth or bandage. Keep pressure until bleeding stops.
  • Burns: Cool the burn under cool (not cold) running water for at least 10 minutes. Do not apply ice.
  • Heart Attack Signs: Chest pain/pressure, shortness of breath, pain in arms/jaw. Call emergency services immediately.
  • Stroke Signs (FAST):
    - Face drooping
    - Arm weakness
    - Speech difficulty
    - Time to call emergency
